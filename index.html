Our Core Services

Our work is made possible by the generosity of donors and the passion of volunteers. We focus on five main service areas:

Disaster Relief

We respond to nearly 60,000 disasters a year, providing shelter, food, and comfort to those affected.

Blood Donation

We supply approximately 40% of the nation's blood and blood components, saving millions of lives.

Lifesaving Skills

We offer training in first aid, CPR, water safety, and more to prepare communities for emergencies.

Support for Military Families

We provide emergency communications and support to military members and their families worldwide.

International Services

We work with the International Red Cross and Red Crescent Movement to serve communities globally.
