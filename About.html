About the American Red Cross

Learn about our history, mission, and impact

Our Mission

The American Red Cross prevents and alleviates human suffering in the face of emergencies by mobilizing the power of volunteers and the generosity of donors.

Our History

Founded in 1881 by Clara Barton, the American Red Cross has a long history of helping people in times of crisis. We are a part of the International Red Cross and Red Crescent Movement, a global network of aid organizations.
